Campus Life

Front Office

Established In 1974

The front office is the closest actual building to the front gate. This is where visitors can check in, where the sick bay is, and where to go if you're late.



Library

Established In 1986

The library is where students can borrow books, return books, and read. There are hundreds (if not thousands) of books here, and it is open at the second half of lunchtime.
